This project about creating a arduino smart plant pot. It uses plastic lid that has led lights, photoresistor, and fan, arduino mega 2560, stepper, 28BYJ-48 stepper motor (with ULN2003A driver board), RTC module, LCD display, buttons, DHT22 humidity/temperature sensor, water sensor, plant pot,straw,string, half cut bottle, and arduino UNO. Main task is for plant pot to water it self on schedule time while ensuring water, humidity, temperature, and light stable. This smart plant pot can be improved by increasing amount space and water it can carry.
