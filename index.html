The goal of this project is to create a smart plant pot using an Arduino microcontroller. The pot is made from a plastic lid that contains LED lights, a photoresistor, and a fan. Additional components include an Arduino Mega 2560, a 28BYJ-48 stepper motor (with driver board ULN2003A), an RTC module, an LCD, buttons, a DHT22 humidity/temperature sensor, a water sensor, a plant pot, a straw, a string, a half-cut bottle, and an Arduino UNO. The primary function of the smart plant pot is to water itself at scheduled intervals while maintaining stable levels of water, humidity, temperature, and light. Future improvements to the design could include increasing the pot's water capacity and growing space. The goal of this project is to create a smart plant pot using Arduino. The main objective is to ensure that the plant pot waters itself at a scheduled time while maintaining stable water, humidity, temperature, and light conditions.
